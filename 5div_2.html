TOWERING PERFORMANCE

Airing the waves of change

Transmission Lines Division, a part of Shirke Group, is an ISO 9001-2008, ISO 14001-2004 (Environmental Management System Standard), ISO 45001:2018 (Occupational Health and Safety Management System Standard) Certified Division.
This division has seamlessly integrated manufacturing process and effective Strategies delivering world-class Hot Dip Galvanized Steel Structures since last 35 years.

Transmission Line Tower / Telecommunication Towers Manufacturing:

The division has its own world class tower manufacturing facility situated at Mundhwa, Pune (Maharashtra) with total tower fabrication and hot dip galvanizing capacity of 30,000 MT per annum. In cellular unidirectional layout, entire material handling is done by environment friendly electrical cranes and trollies on rails.

This tower manufacturing facility is approved by PGCIL, MSETCL, TANTRANSCO, GETCO, TSTRANSCO, ABB, NTPC, NPCIL, BHEL, Bharti Infratel, Indus Towers, DIL, ACB India Ltd., Suzlon, Reliance etc.


EPC/Turney Construction of EHV transmission lines:

This division execute Turnkey/EPC contracts of EHV Electrical Transmission Lines which include Design, Engineering, Fabrication & Hot Dip Galvanizing on Towers, Supply of all transmission line materials, Survey, Civil Foundations, Erection, Stringing, Testing and Commissioning for Central, State Power Utilities and Private Customers. Till date, the division has successfully completed and commissioned more than 1127 Circuit Kms Extra High Voltage Power Transmission Line projects for our esteemed customers in India.

Presently, the division is executing prestigious project comprising 765 kV D/C (Hexa), 765 kV S/C (Quad), 400 kV D/C (Quad) and 400 kV D/C (Twin) transmission lines for Power Grid Corporation of India Limited (Central Power Utility of India) in Maharashtra.

Manufacturing Capabilities & Capacity

  • Fabrication: 30,000 MT per annum.
  • Galvanizing: 30,000 MT per annum.

Certifications


The ISO 9001-2015, ISO 14001-2015 (Environmental Management System Standard), OHSAS 18001: 2007 (Health and Safety Management System Standard) certified division has seamlessly integrated manufacturing process and effective Strategies delivering world-class Fabricated and Galvanized Steel Structures for last 35 years. Our ultra modern manufacturing plant is located in Pune, Maharashtra, India.
