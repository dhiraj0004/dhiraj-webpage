ONE STOP SOLUTION FOR GRAIN STORAGE

POST HARVEST EQUIPMENTS DIVISION

B.G.Shirke Construction Technology Pvt Limited – PHE Division is pioneer in understanding customer requirement of grain storage, design, testing, manufacturing, supply, erection & commissioning of Galvanized Iron Corrugated (read as GIC) SILO system.

 

The Group is ISO 9001/2000 certified. We are leaders in several fields and have industrialized Civil Engineering Technology. The PHE Division is exclusively engaged in post-harvest engineering, GIC (Galvanized Iron Corrugated) silo storage system for food grains. Silos offered range in capacities from 5 MT to 12500 MT each. PHE Division offers the latest GIC silo storage systems for bulk storage of food grains with complete Grain handling and Cleaning equipment’s capacities ranging from 5 TPH to 350 TPH.

In the last 30 years, we have successfully installed more than 600 GIC silo systems within the country and overseas as well, amounting to 2 million tonnes of food grain storage.

GIC SILO Categories:

  1. Flat Bottom Type: Ranging from 500 MT to 12500 MT
  2. Hopper Bottom Type: Ranging from 5 MT to 1000 MT

A dedicated team of project Engineers who ensure the erection and commissioning of the entire system in committed & shortest possible lead time.

We cater to the following industries in providing customized SILO systems, grain handling equipments (elevators & conveyers), and cleaning devices (Pre-cleaners) & making this entire system centrally controlled via PLC/ MCC & Cables:

  1. Solvent Extraction plants
  2. Cattle / Poultry Feed plant
  3. Grain Based Distilleries
  4. Grain Storage for FCI
  5. Food Mega Park
  6. Wheat Flour Mills & Rice Mills
